---

Use this skill when the user asks for voice, music, ambience, sound design, or lip-sync.

Rules:
- Separate creative audio direction from actual generation.
- Read project context before generating or updating persisted audio.
- Paid generation, episode-wide generation, and lip-sync require approval.
- Do not invent voice ids, media ids, or audio URLs.
